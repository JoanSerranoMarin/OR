This file lists the Ballesteros–Weinstein anchor positions used to define the regions of interest for the GPCR cavity-volume analyses. Within each BW-defined region, a membrane-aware three-dimensional grid was generated. Grid points overlapping protein atoms were excluded, and the remaining free voxels were retained only when they satisfied a local enclosure criterion, removing points exposed to bulk solvent or lipid-facing space. The cavity volume was calculated as the number of retained voxels multiplied by the volume of an individual voxel.
